Hello (Bonjour),

This website is about Canada's diverse Culture and Religion.

Canada is the first country that establishes institutional official multiculturalism. The Canadian Multiculturalism Act affirms the Government of Canada's policy of ensuring that each Canadian is treated fairly by a government that respects and celebrates diversity. The legislation also acknowledges and protects Canada's multicultural heritage.

The purpose of Multiculturalism was to preserve every citizen's cultural liberty and also acknowledge the cultural achievements of diverse ethnic groups in Canadian society. The commission's recommendations were implemented as the government's official multiculturalism policy. Canada's Multiculturalism Policy allows all persons to practise their religions peacefully. This demonstrates the Canadian government's strong commitment to recognizing and respecting its diverse community. The changes to the legislation demonstrate the government's strong promise to multicultural society by permitting people of faith to worship and assemble without restriction or intervention. As a result, it fosters a multifaith society in which individuals respect and accept one another's beliefs and faiths.
